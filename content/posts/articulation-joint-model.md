I was wondering exactly how the articulation joint of the RoBug prototype would work, so I built a model of it in Fusion 360. Everything is to scale with the hoverboard motors and 3030 aluminum extrusion. You can see how the joint will work in providing both articulation and roll compliance between the two halves of the frame.
